MachineX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

MachineX collects the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Location (city)
  • Machine and contract details

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable machine rental and tracking features
  • Manage contracts and payments
  • Improve app functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using cloud infrastructure. Our backend is hosted on Render and data is stored securely in a database.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Information may be shared only as required to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

5. User Rights

Users may request account deletion or data removal by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at:

Email: machinexhelpdesk@gmail.com

7. Account Deletion

Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their MachineX account and associated data at any time.

To request account deletion, please send an email to: machinexhelpdesk@gmail.com

Please include your registered phone number in the email so we can identify your account.

After receiving the request, we will permanently delete your account and associated data from our servers within 7 days.

Data that will be deleted includes:

  • User profile information
  • Phone number and email
  • Machine listings
  • Contracts and activity data
